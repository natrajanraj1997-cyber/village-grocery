Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 12, 2026

Village Grocery ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Village Grocery mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

To provide our delivery service, we collect the following personal information:

  • Name: To identify you.
  • Phone Number: To contact you regarding your order and for login verification (OTP).
  • Delivery Address: To deliver groceries to your location.
  • Profile Picture (Optional): If you choose to upload a photo, we store it to personalize your profile.

2. Permissions We Request

Our app may request access to the following device features:

  • Location: We request your live location only when you click "Share Location" to help us find your delivery address accurately.
  • Camera/Gallery: We request access to your camera or gallery only when you choose to upload a profile picture.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely for:

  • Processing and delivering your orders.
  • Improving our app service.
  • Customer support.

4. Data Security

We prioritize your security. We do not share your personal data with third parties, advertisers, or unauthorized entities, except as strictly necessary for delivery logistics.
